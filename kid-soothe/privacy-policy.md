# Privacy Policy for KidSoothe

**Last updated: December 31, 2025**

## Overview

KidSoothe is a simple watchOS app that provides timed dark mode to help soothe kids to sleep.

## Data Collection

KidSoothe does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information.

- No personal data is collected
- No usage analytics
- No tracking
- No cookies
- No third-party services
- No network connections

The app runs entirely on your Apple Watch and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Data Storage

The only data stored is your timer preference, which is saved locally on your Apple Watch using standard system preferences. This data never leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

KidSoothe is designed to be used by parents to help soothe children. The app does not collect any information from children or any users.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
